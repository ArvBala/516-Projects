What in the Wordle

A project to make word games a little less fun by using computers instead of your brain! Your friends will love this (they will not)!

What's the best starting word?

Look, the intention of this project was never to build a Wordle bot. Everyone knows computers are good at games and they are (and always will) be better than you. You can't trash talk a computer, but you CAN trash talk your friends-- and it helps if you're better than them at the game. I wanted to do a bit of math to help me determine the bestle starting wordle.
I didn't use the official Wordle list, but the underlying concepts are all the same. I did this analysis in Python. If you prefer R, my advice to you is to simply not prefer R.
